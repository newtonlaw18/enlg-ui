full ingredients

A multifunctional all-in-one skin treatment that exfoliates, clarifies, and hydrates in one step. Reduces the appearance of pores and blemishes such as blackheads, whiteheads and pimples. Provides gentle yet effective exfoliation, improving skin texture and even skin tone.

For individuals who want effortless skincare that works.

texture & pH

Weightless, transparent gel. pH 3.70, slightly lower than your skin's pH.
May not be suitable for irritated skin.

direction of use

Apply once or twice daily after cleansing. Best applied to skin in the evening. Like any skincare product, apply before sunscreen if used in the morning.

If you're feeling oily If you're feeling dry If you're feeling sensitive If you're new to AHAs

This All-Rounder’s weightless texture is great for your skin needs. Apply up to three pea-sized pumps across the whole face in AM/PM.

This All-Rounder contains humectants which hydrate your skin. Apply until desired moisture is achieved but take note of sensitivity as a result of over-applying. If your skin is still feeling dry after applying, consider combining with the All-Rounder for dehydrated skin.

Apply one drop for the whole face. It's normal to experience some light stinging. However, if you're still stinging a lot, rinse off with water immediately. In that case, use it as a wash-off mask once a week.
Contact us if you have any questions.

Start slow by applying a thin layer across the face. Tingling sensation is normal and will gradually lessen with continuous use.

sun protection advice

This All-Rounder contains glycolic acid, which may increase your skin's sensitivity to the sun and possibility of sunburn. However, the chance of irritation is greatly reduced when a sunscreen rated SPF 50 or greater is applied everyday. Avoid prolonged sun exposure and seek shade when outdoors.
